# Community and External Surfaces
*(Where AI systems actually learn)*

## TL;DR
AI systems do not learn only from your site.

They learn from:
- Communities
- Reviews
- Forums
- Independent content

Ignoring these surfaces cedes control.

---

## 1. Why communities matter

Communities:
- Use natural language
- Ask real questions
- Provide comparative context

AI systems value this data highly.

---

## 2. High-impact surfaces

Examples:
- Reddit
- Quora
- Independent blogs
- Review platforms
- Wikipedia (when appropriate)

---

## 3. Participation vs manipulation

The goal is not spam.
The goal is:
- Accurate representation
- Helpful contribution
- Consistent presence

---

## 4. Practical guidance

We should:
- Monitor key surfaces
- Correct inaccuracies
- Contribute where genuinely helpful
